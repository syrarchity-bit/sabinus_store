What we do — and do not — collect

  • This is an informational website. We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking or profiling.
  • We set no tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for the single functional item stored in your browser.
  • All fonts and assets are served from our own domain. No third-party services (such as font or script CDNs) are contacted, so simply viewing the site does not transmit your data to any third party.
  • We do not operate a web contact form. You contact us only if you choose to email or call us.

Server log files

Our hosting provider automatically processes technical access data (such as IP address, browser type, referring page and the time of the request) in server log files. This is necessary for the secure, stable and efficient operation of the website. Legal basis: our legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR. These logs are not combined with other data or used to identify you.

Contacting us by email or phone

If you contact us, we process the details you provide (such as your name, email address and the content of your message) solely to handle your enquiry. Legal basis: Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (steps prior to a contract) and/or Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (our legitimate interest in responding). We retain such correspondence only as long as necessary and as required by applicable law.

Recipients and international transfers

We do not sell or share your personal data. Data may be processed by our hosting provider acting as our processor for the purpose of operating the website. We do not intentionally transfer personal data outside the EU/EEA through this website.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and to object to processing. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@sabinus.store. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority (in Slovakia, the Office for Personal Data Protection / Úrad na ochranu osobných údajov).

This privacy policy is a starting template that reflects how the website currently works. If you add forms, analytics, embedded media or other services later, it must be updated. Please have it reviewed and finalised by a qualified legal professional for your specific situation and target markets.
