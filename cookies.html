Cookie Policy

Last updated 14 June 2026

Short version

This website uses no tracking, advertising or analytics cookies, and contacts no third-party services when you browse it. There is therefore nothing to consent to for tracking purposes.

The one thing we store

When you dismiss our privacy notice, your browser stores a single entry in its local storage:

  • Key: kn-notice — Value: ok
  • Purpose: so the notice does not reappear on every page you visit.
  • Type: strictly functional. It contains no personal data, is never transmitted to us or anyone else, and is not used to track or identify you.

Because it is strictly necessary for a function you requested (dismissing the notice), no separate consent is required for it under the ePrivacy rules.

How to remove it

You can clear this entry at any time by clearing your browser’s site data / storage for this website, or by using your browser’s privacy settings.

If this changes

If we ever introduce analytics, embedded media or other tools that set non-essential cookies, we will update this page and request your consent beforehand where the law requires it.
