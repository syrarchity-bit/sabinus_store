The company

A focused distributor, built for the region

SABINUS s.r.o. is a Slovak distribution company based in Bratislava. We exist to do one thing well: make a trusted product easy to find, buy and re-stock across Central & Eastern Europe.

Our brand is Kaiser-Natron®, the pure sodium-bicarbonate range that German and Central-European households have relied on since 1825. Working in partnership with ENERGEIA SWISS AG, we handle import, regional logistics and retailer support so that shops, pharmacies, wholesalers and online sellers across the region have a single, dependable point of contact.

From the original pure natron to a full household, cleaning, laundry and care range, we keep stock close and re-orders simple — so the products are always on the shelf when shoppers reach for them.

The original

Kaiser-Natron® — one ingredient, countless uses

Kaiser-Natron® is pure sodium bicarbonate, better known as baking soda or bicarbonate of soda. It has been a fixture of Central-European kitchens and cupboards since 1825.

Chemically, it is mildly alkaline: it binds and neutralises acids, turning them into neutral salts and gentle carbonic acid. That single, simple property is what makes it so useful in the kitchen, around the home and in everyday care. One ingredient, no additives — and it is gluten-free, lactose-free and vegan.

For SABINUS partners, it is an easy product to stock: a recognised name, a long shelf life, stable demand and a story shoppers already trust.

Everyday versatility

What people reach for it

Baking

A classic leavening agent for cakes, breads and pastries, and a help in softening fibrous vegetables and legumes.

Cleaning

A gentle, non-scratching abrasive for sinks, cookware and surfaces — without harsh chemicals.

Odour control

Absorbs and neutralises odours in the fridge, bins, shoes, cars and laundry.

Water & laundry

Softens hard water and freshens fabrics, boosting the effect of everyday detergents.

Personal care

A traditional, mild base used in home care routines — from gentle scrubs to soothing baths.

Antacid

Sodium bicarbonate is a long-recognised aid for neutralising stomach acid. Always follow the directions on the pack.
